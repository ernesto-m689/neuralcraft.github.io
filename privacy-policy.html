1. Overview

NeuralCraft ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered development solutions including web applications, mobile apps, and automation services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information across all our services.

2. Information We Collect

Core Information (All Services)

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you contact us
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our services (anonymized when possible)
  • Communications: Messages you send us for support or inquiries

Development Projects

  • Project Requirements: Business needs, technical specifications
  • Content: Any content you provide for website/app development
  • Feedback: Your input during development and testing phases

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide the specific services you've requested
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improvement: To enhance our services and develop new features
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Security: To protect our services and users from fraud or abuse

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and development tools (under strict agreements)
  • Government APIs: When using official APIs (USCIS, etc.) as specified in service addendums
  • Financial Partners: When providing financial services (details in financial addendum)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process

We never:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share information for marketing purposes without consent
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to our services

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
  • Industry Standards: Compliance with relevant security frameworks

6. Data Retention

  • Project Data: Retained for duration of project plus 1 year for support
  • Communications: Support emails retained for 2 years
  • Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained longer if required by law

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Withdraw consent for non-essential processing

8. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage (can be disabled)
  • No Advertising: We don't use cookies for advertising or tracking across sites

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our website. Service-specific addendums may be updated independently.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Please contact us to exercise these rights or for more information.
